What is kehrn?


kehrn is a volunteer's trusted companion throughout the entire clinical trial journey. It seamlessly integrates with the volunteer's care team, offering an extra layer of support to the volunteer.



With kehrn, there is no need for volunteers to navigate complex medical jargon or wait to consult with healthcare providers. kehrn delivers information in a friendly, accessible way, making the clinical trial experience smoother and more participant-focused.
